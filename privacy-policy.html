WordAdio Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to WordAdio! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our word puzzle game.

Quick Summary: WordAdio is designed with privacy in mind. We don't collect personal information. Game progress is stored locally on your device. We display ads through Google AdMob.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

WordAdio stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Game progress (current level, score, completed levels)
  • Found words and bonus words for your current level
  • App preferences (such as sound/music mute settings)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Advertising Data

WordAdio displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data to provide personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data for ad performance

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google's use of advertising data is governed by their privacy policy. You can learn more at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Free Dictionary API: When you tap on a completed word to see its definition, we fetch the definition from dictionaryapi.dev. Only the word itself is sent to this service.

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, WordAdio will request your permission before allowing advertisers to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites. You can choose to allow or deny this request. Denying tracking will result in less personalized ads but will not affect the game's functionality.

You can change your tracking preferences at any time in your device's Settings under Privacy & Security > Tracking.

Children's Privacy

WordAdio is a general audience word puzzle game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The game does not require account creation or any personal information to play.

Data Security

Since game data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security features. We do not have access to your game progress or settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your data: Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored game data
  • Opt out of personalized ads: You can deny tracking permission or adjust ad personalization in your device settings
  • Reset advertising identifier: You can reset your advertising ID in your device's privacy settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
